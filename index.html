Flowmodoro Guide

The Flowmodoro method is a study technique that combines focused work periods with short breaks to maximize productivity. Here's how to use the Flowcafé app to implement this method:

  1. Set a micro-goal you want to work on.
  2. Select your desired break ratio (we recommend 1/5!) and press the "Start" button to begin the timer.
  3. Achieve your micro-goal.
  4. Press the "Take a Break" button to start your break. The break time will correspond to the break ratio you selected previously.
  5. After the break, the app will automatically start a new work session.
  6. Press the "End" button to reset the timer and end the session.
